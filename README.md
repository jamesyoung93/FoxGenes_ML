# FoxGenes_ML

Data Sources / References:

1. Lechno-Yossef et al., 2011
Title: Mutations in genes encoding putative membrane proteins cause defective diazotrophic growth in the heterocyst-forming cyanobacterium Anabaena sp. strain PCC 7120
Journal: Journal of Bacteriology
DOI: 10.1128/JB.05134-11

Established ~10 FOX genes via insertional mutagenesis.

Defines the foundational FOX vs NotFOX gene reference sets.

2. Tiruveedhula & Wangikar, 2017
Title: Gene essentiality and regulatory analysis in Anabaena sp. PCC 7120 under diazotrophic conditions
Journal: Biotechnology and Bioengineering
DOI: 10.1002/bit.26453

Computational and regulatory network modeling to suggest additional FOX genes.

Introduced the concept of conserved NotFOX sets for benchmarking.

3. Flaherty et al., 2011
Title: Global transcriptional response of Anabaena sp. strain PCC 7120 to nitrogen deprivation
Journal: Journal of Bacteriology
DOI: 10.1128/JB.01325-10

Time-series RNA-Seq under N-deprivation: 0h, 6h, 12h, 21h.

Key input for transcriptomic-based FOX prediction.

4. Zhang et al., 2021
Title: Proteomic analysis of Anabaena sp. PCC 7120 reveals protein dynamics under nitrogen deprivation
Journal: Frontiers in Microbiology
DOI: 10.3389/fmicb.2021.643382

Provided protein fold-changes and p-values under N-starvation.

Useful for integration into machine learning FOX classifiers.

🧬 Large-Scale Screens & Resources
5. Rubin et al., 2015
Title: High-throughput mutant phenotyping reveals key gene functions in cyanobacteria
Journal: eLife
DOI: 10.7554/eLife.07102

While not focused only on Anabaena, it includes mutant fitness data across various conditions.

Good for broader cyanobacterial functional gene insights.

6. Zhang et al., 2009
Title: A genome-scale analysis of the Anabaena sp. PCC 7120 transcriptome under nitrogen-replete and nitrogen-depleted conditions
Journal: Molecular Genetics and Genomics
DOI: 10.1007/s00438-009-0475-z

Another valuable transcriptomic dataset with FOX-related expression data.
